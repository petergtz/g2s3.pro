How does G2S3 Pro work?
G2S3 uses Google Takeout to export your data from Google Drive. It then uploads the data to Amazon S3. You can schedule G2S3 Pro to run periodically to keep your backup up-to-date.

How much will G2S3 Pro cost?
G2S3 Pro will be free for up to 15GB and the first 12 months. After that, it will cost 1 EUR/month for 15GB, 2 EUR/month for 1TB, and 4 EUR/month for 2TB. We will also offer yearly plans.
