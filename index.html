Do you have a Google account with important data? Like family pictures in Google Photos, important documents in Google Drive, archived emails in Gmail? Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data in my Google account not secure?
Google has a good track record of keeping your data secure. However, there is a risk of being locked out of your account and you lose access to your data.

Why would I be locked out of my Google account?
There are many reports on the Internet how Google locked people out of their accounts, allegedly for child sexual abuse material (CSAM), suspicious activities, harmful content etc. See e.g. The New York Times, Reddit, Hacker News, Quora and many other places. Even with proof that no terms of service were violated, people often don't get their accounts back.

Does G2S3 Pro help me to get my account back?
No, G2S3 Pro does not help you to get your account back. However, it helps you to limit the blast radius of a potential lockout. You can still access your data in Amazon S3. You can also use the data to create a new Google account and import the data.

How does G2S3 Pro work?
G2S3 Pro regularly scans your Google Drive for changes and copies them over to Amazon S3. To back your Google Photos and other Google data, we will leverage Google Takeout. Takeout exports your data into Google Drive, where G2S3 Pro can conveniently pick it up. We will provide smart options to handle the takeouts appropriately.

How much will G2S3 Pro cost?
G2S3 Pro will be cheaper than any other storage solution out there. It wil be free for up to 15GB and the first 12 months. After that, it will cost 1 EUR/month for 15GB, 2 EUR/month for 1TB, and 4 EUR/month for 2TB. We will also offer yearly plans that provide further discounts.
